# Succession and Handover Plan

## Before operational handover

- appoint a Principal Administrator;
- train at least two Operations Administrators;
- inventory GitHub, cloud, DNS, email, WhatsApp and backup ownership;
- document recurring tasks;
- run a recovery exercise;
- confirm privacy and data-retention responsibilities;
- record the handover decision in an executive meeting minute.

## Founder role after handover

The Founder Guardian should not perform daily collections or routinely access household information. The role becomes governance, continuity and emergency recovery.

## If the founder becomes unavailable

Executives should use a documented multi-party succession procedure. No recovery secret should depend on one unavailable individual indefinitely.
