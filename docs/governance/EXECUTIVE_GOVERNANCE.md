# EcoRevive OS Executive Governance Charter

## Reserved authorities

### Founder Guardian

The Founder Guardian is the long-term constitutional steward of EcoRevive OS. The initial identity is:

- Name: Jaiwal Patel
- Email: `ujaiwal@outlook.com`

Reserved authority includes appointing the Principal Administrator, reviewing governance audit events, responding to administrator compromise and approving platform-ownership transfers.

### Founder Recovery

EcoRevive OS may maintain one dormant Founder Recovery identity. Its existence must be disclosed to the executive leadership and recorded in the governance register. It is excluded from routine operational user-management views, but it is not hidden from authorized governance or security review.

Its intended scope is limited to:

- restoring the Founder Guardian identity;
- freezing compromised administrative access;
- appointing a temporary Principal Administrator;
- reviewing governance-level audit events.

It must not be used for routine operations or unrestricted browsing of resident data.

## Operational delegation

The Principal Administrator manages daily operations, staff access, collection workflows and ordinary configuration. Operations Administrators receive only the permissions needed for collection coordination.

## Emergency-control requirements

Before a Founder Recovery identity is enabled:

1. use a separate email address not used for ordinary operations;
2. protect it with a passkey or hardware security key;
3. store recovery material offline;
4. configure out-of-band alerts for every use;
5. test access at least twice per year;
6. document all emergency uses and post-incident review.

## Limitations

Application-level protections do not make an identity technically unremovable by a person who controls the production database, source repository, cloud root account, domain and backups. Ownership of those layers must be governed separately.
